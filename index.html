HOW TO PLAY
⟳
Tap anywhere to flip gravity. Your dot flies up or down — that's the only control.
◆
Fly through the gaps and grab the glowing gems. Each gem = 1 point.
✦
Grab gems back-to-back to build a streak. Miss one and the streak resets.
⬡
Every 10 streak enters a new zone — colors shift and your coin multiplier climbs.
⚡
Grab power-ups: slow-mo, shield (one free hit), magnet, and 2× coins.
▰
Lasers block half the gap, then switch sides — watch the orange warning and flip to safety.
$
Points become coins at game over. Spend them in the shop on skins, trails, themes & more.
★
Try Levels for hand-built challenges, or Zen mode to practice with no death.
